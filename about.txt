Evanescent Totes is a Junior Achievement Company run by hard-working and committed students.
Our mission statement, “We strive to combine versatility and creativity to make an everyday, on the go, quality totebag to aid our customers with convinience and fashion”. Our Goal as a company is to create the ultimate tote bag that combines everything you could want. Our bags are reuseable, light, tough, fashionable, and clean for the enviroment!
